About the Firm

The Legal Source
& Associates

Advocate Md Hasan Milu is the Founder and Team Leader of The Legal Source & Associates, a multidisciplinary legal and corporate consultancy firm based in Dhaka. The firm serves startups, SMEs, NGOs and multinational companies with practical, result-oriented legal solutions.

With extensive experience in litigation, corporate governance, company law and regulatory compliance, the firm is committed to delivering ethical, confidential and timely counsel.
